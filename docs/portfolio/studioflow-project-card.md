# StudioFlow Portfolio Project Card

## Project Card Title

StudioFlow

## Project Card Subtitle

Full-stack SaaS platform for service businesses

## Short Card Copy

StudioFlow is a multi-location SaaS platform for appointment-based service businesses. It combines an internal operations workspace for bookings, clients, staff, payments, consent forms, notifications, analytics, and audit history with a separate public booking and customer self-service flow.

## Medium-Length Card Copy

StudioFlow is a production-minded full-stack SaaS platform built for service-led businesses such as salons, tattoo studios, and wellness practices. It includes a premium internal dashboard, calendar-driven appointment workflows, payments, consent forms, notifications, audit history, public booking, customer self-service management, multi-location support, and deployment-ready environment configuration.

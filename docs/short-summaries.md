# StudioFlow Short Summaries

## One-Sentence Description

StudioFlow is a full-stack multi-location SaaS platform for service-led businesses, combining internal operations workflows with customer-facing booking and self-service management.

## Two-Sentence Description

StudioFlow is a production-minded SaaS product for businesses like salons, barbershops, tattoo studios, and wellness practices. It includes internal scheduling, clients, payments, consent forms, reminders, analytics, audit history, and a separate public booking plus self-service manage-booking flow.

## Short Portfolio Summary

StudioFlow is a multi-location appointment operations platform that brings booking, client management, payments, consent, reminders, analytics, and audit visibility into one product. It also includes a customer-facing booking and self-service experience, making it a stronger full-stack SaaS case study than an internal dashboard alone.

# StudioFlow Resume Packaging

## One-Line Summary

Built StudioFlow, a full-stack multi-location SaaS platform for service-based businesses with internal operations workflows and customer self-service booking.

## Two-Sentence Summary

StudioFlow is a production-minded SaaS platform for service-led businesses such as salons, barbershops, tattoo studios, and wellness teams. It combines internal scheduling, clients, payments, consent, notifications, analytics, audit history, and multi-location support with a separate public booking and self-service management experience.

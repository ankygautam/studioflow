# StudioFlow Short-Form Demo Assets

## 30-Second Screen Recording

### Goal

Give a recruiter or reviewer a fast, high-signal understanding of what StudioFlow is.

### Screen Order

1. Login
2. Dashboard
3. Calendar
4. Public booking confirmation

### What To Say

"StudioFlow is a full-stack SaaS platform for service businesses. It combines an internal operations workspace for scheduling, clients, payments, consent, notifications, analytics, and audit visibility with a separate customer-facing booking and self-service flow."

### Do Not Waste Time On

- filtering tables
- form typing
- onboarding steps
- audit details beyond one quick proof point

### Ideal Opening Line

"This is StudioFlow, a multi-location operations platform for appointment-based service businesses."

### Ideal Closing Line

"It’s built as a real full-stack SaaS product, not just a dashboard mockup."

## 60-Second Screen Recording

### Goal

Show both internal operations and customer self-service in one coherent minute.

### Screen Order

1. Login
2. Dashboard
3. Location switch
4. Calendar and appointment drawer
5. Payments or consent
6. Public booking
7. Manage booking

### What To Say

"StudioFlow is a multi-location SaaS platform for service-led businesses like salons, tattoo studios, and wellness practices. Internally it handles appointments, clients, staff, payments, consent forms, notifications, analytics, and audit history. Externally it gives customers a public booking flow and a secure self-service reschedule or cancel experience."

### What Not To Show

- long typing sequences
- multiple weak records
- deep settings editing

### Ideal Opening Line

"StudioFlow is built to run the day-to-day operations of appointment-first businesses."

### Ideal Closing Line

"The product combines internal workflows, customer self-service, and role-aware backend architecture in one system."

## 2-Minute Product Walkthrough

### Goal

Tell a believable product story with enough depth to feel substantial.

### Screen Order

1. Login
2. Dashboard
3. Calendar and one strong appointment
4. Clients, staff, and services
5. Payments
6. Notifications or audit logs
7. Public booking
8. Manage booking
9. Analytics

### What To Say

"StudioFlow is a full-stack SaaS platform for multi-location service businesses. The product is built around daily operational workflows: bookings, customers, staff, services, payments, consent, reminders, notifications, analytics, and audit visibility. Customers never enter the internal dashboard. Instead, they use a separate public booking flow and a secure self-service management flow."

### What Not To Waste Time On

- every sidebar module
- every filter state
- minor settings sections

### Ideal Opening Line

"Let me show you StudioFlow from the perspective of both the internal team and the customer."

### Ideal Closing Line

"That combination of internal operations plus customer self-service is what makes StudioFlow feel like a real SaaS product."
